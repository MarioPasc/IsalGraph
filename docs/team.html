Ezequiel López-Rubio

Principal Investigator

Full Professor
Dept. of Computer Languages and Computer Science
University of Málaga

Ezequiel López-Rubio is a Full Professor at the University of Málaga. His research interests include graph theory, machine learning, computer vision, and neural network architectures. He is the creator of the IsalGraph framework and the principal investigator of the ICAI research group.

Mario Pascual González

PhD Student

Dept. of Computer Languages and Computer Science
University of Málaga

Mario Pascual González is a PhD student at the University of Málaga working on graph representation learning and the IsalGraph framework. His research focuses on leveraging instruction string encodings for deep learning applications in graph generation, classification, and comparison.

ICAI Research Group

Inteligencia Computacional y Análisis de Imágenes (Computational Intelligence and Image Analysis) is a research group at the University of Málaga focused on machine learning, computer vision, and computational intelligence methods.

The group develops novel algorithms for image and graph analysis, with applications ranging from biomedical imaging to network science.
